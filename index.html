Our Story

Family Heritage Since 1952

From our family's kitchen in Tuscany to your table, we bring authentic Italian flavors and warm hospitality to every meal.

Founded by Nonna Isabella in 1952, Bella Vista began as a small trattoria in the hills of Chianti. What started as a way to share our family's traditional recipes has grown into a beloved destination for authentic Italian cuisine.

Today, our fourth-generation kitchen is led by Chef Marco Rossini, Isabella's great-grandson, who trained in the finest restaurants of Rome and Florence before returning to honor his family's legacy.

Every dish tells a story - from our hand-rolled pasta made fresh daily to our wood-fired pizzas baked in our imported stone oven. We source the finest ingredients, many directly from our family's suppliers in Italy, to ensure each meal captures the true essence of Tuscan cooking.
